Scrutiny

Parliament will also use information from:

  • Public servants;
  • Officers of Parliament, such as the Auditor General; or
  • Parliamentary officials, such as the Parliamentary Budget Officer,

to help it hold ministers to account and decide whether or not to approve the government’s funding request.

Indeed, this scrutiny of the Estimates by Treasury Board before it is brought to Parliament, is intended to ensure the Estimates will withstand the scrutiny of Parliament. If Parliament supports the government’s funding request, it passes the appropriation act.
