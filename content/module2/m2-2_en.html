What Comes After the Election?

Good question. Select each item to uncover the key first steps following an election.

Parties

Parties are elected on the basis of their platforms—that is, what they undertake to do if elected.

The leader

The leader of the party that wins a plurality of seats or “ridings” (i.e. the most seats among all the parties), seeks the approval of the of the Crown through the governor general to form the government.

The government’s agenda

The government’s agenda, sometimes referred to as the “priorities,” will be based on its election platform. Advice from the public service on the transition is also considered.

The agenda

The agenda is communicated in the Speech from the Throne, when Parliament resumes.
