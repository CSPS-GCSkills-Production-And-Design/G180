The term Privy Council

The term Privy Council effectively means Cabinet and the Privy Council Office is the department that supports the prime minister, the Cabinet committees (with the exception of Treasury Board), and the leaders of government in the House and Senate.

The Treasury Board Secretariat

The Treasury Board Secretariat is the department that supports the Treasury Board (one of the Cabinet committees).
