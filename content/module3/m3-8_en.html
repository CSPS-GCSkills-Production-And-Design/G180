Engagement

Select the 2 symbols to read about different sources of advice.

External Sources

Two-way conversations or consultations with sources outside of the government can contribute to understanding an issue more fully, identifying possible solutions, and determining the effect of various options on various interests.

Internal Sources

Internal engagement is important to ensuring the government is coordinated, its policies are coherent, resources are appropriately allocated, etc. These internal engagement objectives are in the collective interest and are largely the focus of central agencies.

Internal consultations focus on the document that will go to Cabinet: the Memorandum to Cabinet.
