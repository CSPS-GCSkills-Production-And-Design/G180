Estimates and Supply Bill

To get the Legislative Branch approval for spending, the Executive Branch must assemble documentation, as follows:

Select the symbols to learn more.

Main Estimates

The Main Estimates (tabled in March), which outline the detailed budgets for each government organization;

Supplementary Estimates

The Supplementary Estimates (tabled up to three times a year), which outline adjustments to departmental “reference levels” (i.e. budgets); and

Supply Bill

A Supply Bill (tabled as part of the Estimates process), which is the draft law allowing spending or changes to taxation.
